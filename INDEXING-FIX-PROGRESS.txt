GOAL
----
Make all articles indexable by Google by fixing three linked problems, all
caused by client-side Sanity fetching in a statically-generated (vite-react-ssg)
app + stale hardcoded slug lists:

  #1 Listing/related widgets render ZERO article links in static HTML (orphaned).
  #2 The 11 prerendered Sanity article pages render an empty loading skeleton
     in static HTML (blank content to Googlebot).
  #3 6 newer Sanity articles are not prerendered and not in the sitemap at all
     (fully invisible).

Root fix: ONE build-time Sanity fetch = single source of truth ("articles
manifest"), unioning Sanity (17) + hardcoded components (the-number-33,
famous-celebrity-birthdays) = 19 articles. That manifest feeds:
  - the sitemap
  - routes.tsx getStaticPaths (so every article is prerendered)
  - the listing/related widgets (so links exist in static HTML)
  - ArticlePage initial content (so article body is in static HTML)

generate-articles (Sanity fetch) -> generate-sitemap -> SSG build. New Sanity

the build never breaks.
